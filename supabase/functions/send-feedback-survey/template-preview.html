Template Structure

The template consists of:

  1. Header: "How did we do?"
  2. Body text with parameters:
    • Business name: Jasper's Market
    • Location: 123 Baker street, Palo Alto CA, 91041
    • Date: Jan 1, 2024
  3. Button: "Fill out survey" (links to survey URL)
